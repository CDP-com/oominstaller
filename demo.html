Install

Click the "Install Button" to install the SnapBack Apps Platform. This will install your app container which includes your featured apps and keeps everything running smoothly on your system.

In doing so, you will be asked to run a .exe file in order to install. This will look different depending upon what type of browser you are using.

Q: What does this app do?
A: It downloads and installs the SnapBack Apps Platform including the app container (MoM) and featured SnapBack Apps.

Q: Is it safe?
A: Yes. CDP certifies the download as safe and secure.

Q: Who is this app for?
A: Everyone! You will need this in order to use SnapBack Apps
